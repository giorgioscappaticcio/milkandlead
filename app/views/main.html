D E L I Q U I U M

DARK MOVEMENTS EXHIBITION

Deliquium is a project that aims to combine music producers and a permanent collective art exhibition involving Dark movements such as Industrial/Dark Ambient/Noise/Electronic/. During the three days we aim to alternate on our stage artists performing with diverse type of media, but united by a strong and nieche identity. Our main scope is to promote good quality music and give the possibility to some selected emerging new talents to exhibit their works aside estabilished artists. In addition, Deliquium have to be considered a platform for visual artists that are exploring the same topics and are connected by the same influences. Therefore, with Deliquium we want our audience to be sank away by the cultural stream of Darkness.

   MILKandLEAD is delighted to announce its first Art Contest, with places in our dark movements exhibition, DELIQUIUM, up for grabs (for details check MILKandLEAD website NEXT section).   The stable exhibition will be the background of a three day music festival from established Noise, Dark Ambient and Electronic artists.   It will take place at the Electrowekz, Angel London from Thursday 19th to Saturday 21st of February.   MILKandLEAD is offering the chance for four visual artists to have their work on show. The selection process has two stages.   Three winners will be selected by a technical jury including such judges as Francesco Vertucci (MILKandLEAD Director), Cliff Wallace (Special Effect Creator) and Iris Schiefersten (Taxidermist).   The winner of first prize will also have their travel expenses and accomodation covered during the exhibition. While second and third   The first prize will be a 3 days travel plus accommodation in London during the exhibition. While second and third prize will be the 3days tickets to the event   Don't miss this chance to bring your work to the next level!
