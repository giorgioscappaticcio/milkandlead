MILKandLEAD is an independent art gallery organisation fouded on October the 22nd 2009 by Francesco Vertucci e Jasmine Balder.
The gallery has been active into the underground movements for over 5 years, exhibiting artists whose sign –we feel- is leaving a unique impression in this moment in history.
After organising different kind of events the gallery started as well with a new different projects MILKandLEAD “secret gallery” and דיאליזה- DIALYSIS (Body Art Performances) exhibiting between huge rich places and decadent squats were the athmosphere reflects the mood of the event. The new gallery space is based in london fields , east London UK.
In 2012 Raffaella Mandriota has joined our team as Managing director and carrying on projects with Francesco Vertucci as Creative director and art curator.

Supporting our research for the discovery of new cultural and artistic forms but never allowing the mainstream to suffocate what differentiates itself from the logic of the masses; usually because it springs out of a simple and honest personal belief.
